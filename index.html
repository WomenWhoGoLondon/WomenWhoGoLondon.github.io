Who Are We

We're a chapter of Women Who Go, which was founded in San Francisco by Sarah Adams in May 2015. Sue Spence (@virtualsue) started this chapter in September 2015. If you identify as a woman, are interested in the Go programming language and can come to one of our meetings, you are most welcome to join us.

Join us on Slack, Meetup and Twitter @womenwhogolndn.
